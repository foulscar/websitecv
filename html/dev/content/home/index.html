Who Am I

I focus on concept design and the deployment of highly configurable, scalable, and available architectures. My journey began when I was 14, tinkering with small projects at school and work. It started with learning JSON and YAML to create addons for the games I played at the time. Shortly after, I began learning the basics of various programming and scripting languages like Python and Bash through Udemy courses. Fast forward to 18, I'm pursuing an Associate's Degree in Cyber Security at Coastal Pines, taking a Networking class with an emphasis on CISCO, and I just fell in love. After discovering my passion for networking, I researched further and enrolled in the "AWS for Jobs" program at YellowTail Tech.
